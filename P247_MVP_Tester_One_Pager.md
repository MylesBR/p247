# P247 Beta Tester Programme
## Looking for 30 serious athletes to test P247

### What is P247?

**The AI performance analyst for serious athletes.**

P247 reads your Garmin, Whoop, Apple Health, Strava, and nutrition data overnight and sends you one clear signal each morning: what your body is actually saying, and what today should look like based on it.

Not a dashboard. Not a training plan. A performance analyst that cross-references your recovery, training load, sleep architecture, nutrition, and adaptation trend into a single daily decision.

**The analogy:** Your wearable is the blood test. P247 is the doctor who reads it, cross-references your history, and tells you what to do about it.

---

### The Problem We're Solving

You wake up, see a green recovery score on Whoop, check Garmin which says yellow, glance at your sleep score, remember you did heavy deadlifts yesterday, and have to make a training decision with conflicting, siloed information.

**The gap is not data collection. It's data interpretation.**

Every wearable tells you what happened. None of them tell you what it means for today.

### Who We're Looking For

We want **serious endurance athletes** who:

- Currently train for **Hyrox, Ironman, marathon, ultra, or cycling events**
- Use at least **2 of these devices/apps:** Garmin, Whoop, Apple Health, Strava, Oura
- Track **nutrition or subjective feel** (even informally)
- Are willing to give **honest feedback** (good and bad)

**Ideal candidates:**
- Training for a specific event in the next 3–6 months
- Currently frustrated with conflicting wearable data
- Open to adjusting training based on data when it makes sense

### The Beta Process

**Week 1: Onboarding**
- Connect your devices (Garmin, Whoop, Apple Health, Strava, nutrition app)
- Brief onboarding call with Myles (15 min) — training background, goals, pain points
- First morning brief delivered within 24 hours

**Weeks 2–4: Daily Briefs + Feedback Loop**
- Morning brief delivered every day before your session
- Weekly check-in (async via Telegram or 10-min call)
- Quick feedback survey after each week

**Week 5: Wrap-up**
- Final feedback session (20 min)
- Option to continue into extended beta (free for 6 months post-launch)
