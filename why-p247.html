Your wearable collects data.
P247 tells you what to do.

Every morning, P247 predicts how your body will respond to today's training and makes one clear decision for you. Not a dashboard of metrics. One call, with reasoning, that gets smarter every time you train.

What P247 Does

☀️

Morning Decision

One decision, delivered before your session. Go hard, pull back, shift focus, or rest. Each decision comes with a confidence level and the reasoning behind it. P247 shows you exactly which signals drove the call so you understand the why, not just the what. Not a dashboard. A decision.

🧠

AI Performance Coach

A personal coaching agent that knows your training history, body composition, injury status, and goals. Ask it anything. Send it a photo of your gym's whiteboard and it analyses the actual workout, connects it to your training phase, and gives specific feedback. It remembers what you told it last week and adapts as your data changes. Not a chatbot with canned responses.

📋

Daily Plan

Your single action screen for the day. Completed workouts, coach-suggested sessions, scheduled events, and recovery alerts in one chronological feed. When your coach suggests a complementary PM session based on your morning workout, it appears as an actionable card with exercises, sets, reps, and a Start button. Tap through each exercise as you go.

📸

Workout Image Analysis

Snap a photo of your gym's whiteboard, your Apple Watch summary, or a Strava screenshot. P247's coach reads the actual workout and coaches you on what it sees. No hallucinated responses, no generic advice. If the whiteboard says row/ski/bike intervals, you get feedback on row/ski/bike intervals, not a made-up running session.

😴

Sleep Analysis

Deep sleep, REM, awakenings, sleep efficiency, and how last night actually affects today's readiness. Your wearable gives you a sleep score. P247 tells you why it matters for your training and what to change. When your sleep drops below threshold, it shows up as a recovery alert in your daily plan.

📊

Training Load Intelligence

CTL, ATL, TSB, and weekly load trends synthesised from every workout across Strava and Apple Health. P247 tracks your chronic and acute training load so you can see whether you're building fitness or digging a recovery hole. Across all your activities, not just one sport.

💪

Body Composition Tracking

Track skeletal muscle mass, body fat percentage, and visceral fat over time. Especially important if you're on GLP-1 medication or cutting weight for competition. See whether you're losing fat or muscle, not just "weight".

🥩

Nutrition & Hydration

Protein, calories, fibre, macros, and daily water intake pulled from your tracking app. P247 correlates your nutrition and hydration with your recovery and training performance so you can see the impact of what you eat and drink, not just what you lift.

❤️

HRV & Recovery

Heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and recovery trends tracked continuously. P247 flags when your recovery is genuinely compromised versus when a single metric is misleading you. Context, not just numbers.

🏃

Running Mechanics

Ground contact time, vertical oscillation, stride length, cadence, and VO2 Max trends. Useful data that most wearables collect but never help you act on. P247 connects your mechanics to your performance trajectory.

🔗

Connect Everything

Native Apple Health sync reads 35+ data types directly from your watch. Strava connects via OAuth for activity and route data. No third-party export apps, no manual spreadsheets, no pasting data into ChatGPT. Your data flows automatically and securely.

🏋️

Strava Activity Feed

All your Strava activities in one feed with distance, pace, heart rate, calories, and effort scores. Weekly summaries show volume, type breakdown, and load distribution. Pull to sync and your latest session appears in seconds.

🔄

Gets Smarter Over Time

Predict. Decide. Act. Measure. Learn. That's the loop. P247 tracks whether you followed its recommendation and what happened next. Did you ignore the "pull back" call and crush it anyway? Or did you push through and crash the next day? The system learns your patterns. Weeks 1 and 2 establish your baseline. By month 3, decisions are deeply personalised to how your body actually responds.

⚠️

Risk Intelligence

Injury risk, burnout risk, and performance suppression are first-class concepts in P247. Named predictions like Fatigue Spillover Risk, Performance Suppression Window, Load Accumulation Risk, and Recovery Instability surface before they become problems. Your wearable tells you what happened. P247 tells you what's about to happen.
