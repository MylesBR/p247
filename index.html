Why P247?

🔍 Synthesis, not data

HRV, sleep quality, training load, and nutrition — cross-referenced and interpreted for you. Not another dashboard.

📲 Proactive, not reactive

Your daily brief arrives before your session. You don't check it — it finds you.

🎯 Analyst, not coach

A coach builds your plan. P247 reads your body and tells you whether to execute it. You keep your program — we tell you what today's readiness actually means for it.

📈 Progress, not just today

Your recovery score tells you about last night. P247 tracks your adaptation over weeks — so you can see your fitness actually building, not just react to each day in isolation.
